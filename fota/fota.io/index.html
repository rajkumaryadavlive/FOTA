The Hidden Key To FOTA

Metaverse

Welcome to The Metaverse - a new era where the real meets the virtual. Not only delivering the best 3D-game experience, FOTA also empowers its players to roleplay and make their characters exclusively theirs in this fantasy world. Integrated with the MOBA-RPG genre, players can real-time interact with their teammates in the FOTA Metaverse. Powered by Mixed Reality from Microsoft.

Microsoft Mesh

With Microsoft Mesh, you are empowered to connect to the FOTA Metaverse anytime, anywhere with any device you are using. Once you get in, you can real-time interact with your team and other players. The FOTA Heroes are from three different worlds: The Greenland, The Earth and The Nightmare.
Why have to cope with geographical boundaries while you can join the FOTA Metaverse? Let's go!

Worlds

Out of this wide, wide FOTA universe, there are many other worlds yet to be explored. But the battle for the Aether still takes place on three worlds in the Unity Saga:
The Greenland: The Land of Eternity, where was once the birthplace of the Aether. The Greenland is home to god-like creatures known as the Omni and Dragon.
The Earth: The Ultimate Civilization. Here the Elf, Human and Keen live together and receive the Omni's preparation for the dark days ahead.
The Nightmare: The Darkest Night, a wasteland of FOTA universe. Cold, dark and cruel, exactly what this world is called - nightmares; only the darkest creatures like the Demon, Ogre and Beast can survive here.

Heroes

Entering into the FOTA Metaverse - A 3D Space, each player will become his unique heroes to start the journey. Each hero is born with his own power, story and mission. In that chaos, will your hero fight for justice to protect the balance of the whole universe? Or will you lead a war of extermination, hope to rid the world of other heroes and claim dominion over it?

Lands

In the era of The Unity Saga, there are total 30 lands on three worlds: The Greenland, The Earth and The Nightmare. Each land is encrypted and assigned a unique NFT. Since then, without the shadow of a doubt, only the strongest heroes can have enough power to take the lead and rule these lands forever.

story

Aether - The ultimate energy source creates all the matters in the FOTA universe. Today, where it once lived is called the Greenland, inhabited by the Omni and the Dragon. The whole universe is yearning to protect the Aether before the onslaught from the Nightmare, with the evil Ogre army led by the Demon. It was in that moment, when all hope had faded, that the Omni came to the Earth to offer alliances to the Elf and the Human. Will the universe strike a balance between these forces? Or will it get swallowed up by the bad?

The Hidden Key To FOTA
CIARA
“Surprise. Now you see me, just in time before your body's even cold”
Ciara is rumored to be a great assassin of the ancient Omni. But because of her brother's greed, she was sent to The Inferno and turned into a demon with the instincts of an assassin. Once an assassin, always an assassin. Right at this very moment, Ciara is the right-hand of Cyril, a haunted obsession of any creature when night falls.
KEISHA
“Your life is mine, dear. Beg for it.”
Being a member of a noble and long-line guild in Mist Island, Keisha has inherited and instilled the ideal of this region - being a cold-blooded assassin, instinctively and senselessly.
He has joined in many battles, including the fiercest ones against The Orge, not for the appeal of King of The Middle-Earth Kingdom, but for his own life ideal.
APRUM
“Kill, or be killed!”
Aprum is a wild and violent warrior of The Beast. He takes the form of a wild boar that is fierce and crazy fighting, even when alone.
Aprum, due to its power, obviously embodies most of The Beast creatures - brutish but stupid.
CYRIL
“You think you know pain? Don't rush, the fun has just begun.”
Cyril - a brutal tyrant blew out of the hell of The Nightmare, but very few know that he was also once a noble Omni.
Legend has it that Cyril was arrogant and thought he could rule the universe. Upon his arriving at The Nightmare, Cyril was enchanted by dark magic, with his bottomless greed, he sold his soul to the devil in exchange for power at The Devil's Bridge and then was reincarnated in The Inferno. Therefore, he is known to be the very embodiment of all that is evil and cruel in this universe.
Eira
“Don't ever, never, ever mess around with my land. I was born with no heart.”
Eira was the descendent of a warrior in the far and cold Northern Walls patrol. This land was full of dangers and the pursuit of dark forces. Therefore, Eira was raised to be a skilled warrior with a high spirit. She was especially special, she mastered in archery and perfectly used bows, arrows to fight and protect herself.
One day, one of the demons of The Nightmare killed her beloved father. Since then, she nursed a grudge in her, wandering across the North to hunt and slaughter any creature coming from The Nightmare with her frozen arrows, just like her heart the day her father left.
alva
“Go and enjoy the light before the dark covers all over you, my sweet little enemy. ”
Alva was a young lady of a noble birth of The Elf, with the power of faith & hope.
She was chosen as the successor to protect the Dreaming Valley where was constantly in the dark forces' sights for its endless energy. To ensure its peace, she decided to become a powerful Mage, which was full of difficulties and temptations. Following the lead of a mysterious shaman from the ancient Omni, she has mastered in using and controlling the enormous energy of the light.
Zel
“My master is the strongest. Guess who? Look, it is I.”
Born in the Magic Lake area, Zel is a unique creature with his special skill in using poison as his ultimate weapons. Combined with its cunning nature, Zel was soon be entrusted and became the right-hand man of Orson.
The collision of the horny skin on his body when moving makes such horrible sounds, brings death and suffering to any creature on his path. They call this "Sound of Death".
kyla
“Do not underestimate the power of the little ones, you will never know what kind of things you are up against. You, kneeling and begging before it's too late.”
Kyla, a creature of the ancient Dragon, lives on the Jade Dragon Snow Mountain on The Greenland. Although not as giant and fierce as other Dragons, little Kyla possesses agility and an endless energy that allows it to attack a large area and heal the allies.
farrer
“Boom! Boom! Boom! Such a sweet sound. That would be one of the last sound you'd hear.”
Farrer - A skilled and elite warrior of The Keen, was born in The Modern City. Therefore, he is heavily armed with the most advanced technology weapons which helps him untouchable and fearless.
No matter how strong he is, it becomes quickly apparent, that he does have a fatal weakness. When fighting in any place with the lack of energy or in intensely magnetic environments, Farrer can not control himself and be easily manipulated by the enemy.
orson
“Only the victor deserves to be the new Alpha. It's me! .”
Orson is a warrior of The Beast with a huge body and very aggressive. He possesses devastating power that threatens all creatures.
Alongside the wild army, Orson sought and conquered all the lands that contains boundless energy to power himself to immortality. His biggest goal is to kill Tigris and bring The Beast under his control.
Recognizing The Beast's getting stronger everyday, Cyril tried to beat them with Kekor's army many times but all in vain. The sooner the better, Orson is determined to kill Kekor at all costs.
gideon
“The blue heaven is screaming, you should leave. Now! ”
Gideon is the Thunder God of The Omni.
Possessing the power of manifesting lighting and tempests, Gideon was the one who helped The Human to stem The Orge army commanded by the devil Cyril. That's the reason why to this day, after so much bloodshed, some lands have been completely destroyed and still engulfed in water.
Today, at some regions of The Earth, he is worshipped as the God of Strength that forbids any vicious creatures setting foot here.
Aedus
“Just a single flash of light can tear down the night sky.”
Aedus was one of the first and most intelligent mages in The Mid-Earth Kingdom. He was entrusted by the king with the task of governing the Sun Temple - a worship place of the gods and the power of Aether. He used the energy here for cultivating to reach the Deep Magic.
In the battle of the century at the Dark Moon, Aedus showed his endless power as the head of the Sun Temple and became the worst enemy of Grimm - A Death God leading the Dark Army of The Nightmare but suffering defeat at the hands of Aedus
